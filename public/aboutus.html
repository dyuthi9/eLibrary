📚 Welcome to Our Library Management System

Our **Library Management System** is designed to make **book borrowing, returning, and tracking** easier and more efficient. Whether you’re a **student, faculty member, or book lover**, this system helps you manage your library interactions seamlessly.

🌟 **How to Use the Library System**

🔹 **For Users (Students & Readers)**

  • **Register/Login** – Sign up with your details or log in to your existing account.
  • **Browse Books** – Search for books by **title, author, or category**.
  • **Borrow Books** – Click the **"Borrow"** button to request a book. The request will be sent to the librarian.
  • **View Borrowed Books** – Check the books you have borrowed and their **due dates**.
  • **Return Books** – Click the **"Return"** button when you're ready to return the book. The admin will approve the return.
  • **Track Fines** – If a book is overdue, a **fine will be calculated** per day. Users can view and manage their fines.

🔹 **For Admins (Librarians & Management)**

  • **Manage Books** – Add, update, and delete book records from the system.
  • **Handle Borrow Requests** – Approve or decline **book borrow requests** from users.
  • **Track Returns** – Approve book return requests and ensure books are returned in good condition.
  • **Monitor Users** – Keep track of registered users, their borrowed books, and overdue fines.

🚀 **Why Use Our Library Management System?**

Our system is designed to **simplify** and **digitize** library management, making it more **efficient, organized, and user-friendly**.

📌 **Key Benefits**

  • ✔️ **Time-Saving** – No need to manually track books; everything is **automated**.
  • ✔️ **Easy Access** – Users can check book availability from anywhere.
  • ✔️ **Fine Management** – Automatically calculates and notifies users about overdue fines.
  • ✔️ **Secure & Reliable** – User data and book records are stored **securely** in a database.
  • ✔️ **User-Friendly Interface** – Simple and intuitive design for both users and admins.
